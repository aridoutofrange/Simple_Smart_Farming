# Simple Smart Farming

## Background
In order to achieve a great harvest, certain conditions needed to be met. Temperature, soil moisture, CO2 level, illuminance and exposed pressure are few of the most used parameters to determine whether the environment meets the certain criteria that needed by the plants to flourish. With this simple smart farming gadget, you can adjust the parameters and if the defined conditions are not fulfilled, it will give you a warning.

## Description
Simple yet adjustable smart farming gadget that can keep your plants to stay in the ideal environment.

## Components Used
<li> One Arduino Uno R3</li>
<li> One Light Dependent Resistor </li>
<li> One Soil Moisture Sensor</li>
<li> One TMP36 Temperature Sensor</li> 
<li> One Gas Sensor</li> 
<li> One Force Sensor</li> 
<li> One Green LED</li>
<li> One Red LED</li> 
<li> One Yellow LED</li> 
<li> Jumper Cables </li> 
<li> One 5V Buzzer</li>
<li> Three 100Ω Resistors</li> 
<li> Five 1kΩ Resistors</li> 
<li> Three 16X2 I2C LCDs</li> 
<li> One NPN BJT</li> 
<li> One DC Motor</li> 
<li> One Voltage Multimeter</li> 
<li> One 5,5 Power Supply</li> 
<li> One 1N4007 Diode</li>
